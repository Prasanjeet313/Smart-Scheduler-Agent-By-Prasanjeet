# Privacy Policy for Smart Scheduler AI

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

## Overview
Smart Scheduler AI is a personal scheduling assistant that helps users manage their Google Calendar through natural language conversation.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What We Collect
- **Google Calendar Access**: We request access to your Google Calendar to read availability and create events
- **Gemini API Key**: You provide your own API key which is stored only in your browser session
- **Meeting Details**: Event titles, dates, times, and attendee information you provide during conversation

### How We Use Your Data
- To check your calendar availability
- To create calendar events as you request
- To provide AI-powered scheduling suggestions

### Data Storage
- **No server-side storage**: All authentication credentials are stored only in your browser session
- **No persistent storage**: We do not store your calendar data, API keys, or personal information on our servers
- **Session-based only**: All data is cleared when you close your browser

### Third-Party Services
- **Google Calendar API**: Used for calendar access and event creation
- **Google Gemini AI**: Used for natural language processing (your API key, your usage)

### Your Rights
- You can disconnect Google Calendar access at any time using the "Disconnect" button
- You can reset your session data at any time using the "Reset Chat" button
- You maintain full control of your calendar data

## Security
- OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure Google Calendar access
- No storage of access tokens beyond your browser session
- No sharing of your data with third parties
